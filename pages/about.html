Our Story

Patibrata began as an idea among friends — what if we could make a difference not by giving from afar, but by living among those we wish to help?

At the end of 2025, we took our first steps into a small village in Lombok. What we found was a community rich in culture, wisdom, and resilience, but their own unique challenges and inspiring ways they overcome them.

We chose to stay. To learn the language, share meals, work in the rice fields, and understand what genuine support looks like.

The Village

Where we live and work

Our base is a small village called Sepakek. The community primarily practices subsistence agriculture — rice, corn, and vegetables. Most families have lived here for generations.
