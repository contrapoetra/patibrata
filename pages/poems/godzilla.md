# Godzilla
### by Dimas Atha Putra

grey hair
hard red shirt
a familiar hairdo
zooming in and out of focus and
you were the one everyone holds dear

perhaps i see why
---
I used to struggle to blend in with new friends, especially if they're already a circle. He would always try to rope me into conversations, never left me hanging, and for some reason always smile or even laugh at my jokes or silly weird things I would say as a joke. I am grateful to be friends with him. 
---
you would drag me by the collar
so that i'm never left behind
and allowed my dumb words tickle you
so that i always felt like i was funny
===

nowadays
i would see your dumb face giggle
every time i let dumb jokes out of my mouth
how simple it is to mold my confidence
but your life isn't just jokes and giggles

---
*Our friend, my dear sister, our family* is the person I wrote ["Sister, Darling"](/poems/sister-darling) about. I've also written about this in that poem. The person I wrote this poem about, is the one who ultimately saved her. He endured a wound below his knees in the process. He's a goddamn hero. I couldn't bear to imagine what would've happened without him.
---
our friend, my dear sister, our family
her life flashed before her eyes
her feet tried to feel the riverbed
as the waters was taking her away from us
and you drew your sword
fought the waters
they drew their weapon
grazed you below the knee
and you could not care less
your eyes are locked on hers
you are her only hope
your paths intercept
and you got her out of the masses
she felt the ground underneath her feet
in utter dread, she held you close
===
