---
No... he didn't die, thankfully. We were close friends because we both play this game called Geometry Dash. In Geometry Dash, we are given a new super hard level (known as *demon levels*) to play every week. At the time of Patibrata, the weekly demon was "Everyday" by Nezzieverse. [Here's a showcase of the level](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=0wkqvkGC674). He invited me to a race, to see who would beat the level first, and I agreed.

A few days after, tragedy struck. The *Roommate* (person I wrote "The Arborist" about) accidentally knocked over his tumbler, which was left open for some reason, and water spilled under his laptop. And consequentially, as you may know, chances of water damage to be recoverable is basically zero, he lost his data, his projects, and of course, his progress on the level we're trying to beat. I ended up beating the level and "won the challenge." Though I know deep down that he should've won instead, because his progress on the level was actually quite fast, he understood the layout of the level pretty quickly. If he didn't have a setback, he would've won the race.
