Who We Are

A small group with a big heart

We are Patibrata — a collective of individuals who believe that real change begins at the grassroots. Our name, derived from Sanskrit, means "one who serves with devotion."

Our Mission

To live among, learn from, and lift up

We don't come with solutions — we come with open hands and open hearts. By immersing ourselves in village life, we discover what communities truly need and work alongside them to create sustainable change.

Where We Serve

Lombok, Indonesia

A rural village nestled between Mount Rinjani and the sea. Here, tradition meets the challenges of modern life, and community remains the heart of everything.

8.6500° S | 116.3249° E
What We Do

Three pillars of presence

01

Education

Teaching in local schools, tutoring children, and creating learning opportunities beyond the classroom.

02

Healthcare

Supporting rural health posts, organizing health awareness campaigns, and connecting villagers with medical resources.

03

Community

Participating in daily village activities, from farming to ceremonies, building genuine bonds with locals.

Our Journey

Not tourists, not outsiders

We live in the village. We eat what the villagers eat. We speak their language, celebrate their festivals, and mourn their losses. We become part of the fabric — temporarily, but genuinely.
